Before entering a battle, you choose a character: a Colombian native animal that represents you on the board. Each character belongs to a **species**, can wear a **skin**, and competes under one of nine **teams** inspired by the faculties of the Universidad Nacional de Colombia (UNAL).

## Species

There are four playable species, each drawn from Colombian native wildlife:

```gdscript game_constants.gd
enum Species {
    BEAR,
    CONDOR,
    CHAMALEON,
    FROG
}
```

  <Card title="Frog" icon="circle" color="#27ae60">

## Skins

Each species has one or more skins that change the character's visual appearance. Skins are purely cosmetic — they do not affect gameplay or card draws.

```gdscript game_constants.gd
const SKINS: Dictionary[Species, PackedStringArray] = {
    Species.BEAR:      ["base", "andean", "black", "grizzly", "panda", "polar"],
    Species.CHAMALEON: ["base"],
    Species.CONDOR:    ["base"],
    Species.FROG:      ["base", "green", "perez"]
}
```

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bear (6 skins)">
    The Bear has the most skin variety, reflecting the global diversity of bear species:

    | Skin | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `base` | Default brown bear appearance |
    | `andean` | Inspired by the spectacled (Andean) bear native to Colombia |
    | `black` | Black bear variant |
    | `grizzly` | North American grizzly variant |
    | `panda` | Giant panda variant |
    | `polar` | Polar bear variant |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Frog (3 skins)">
    The Frog has three skins, two of which reference real Colombian species:

    | Skin | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `base` | Default green frog appearance |
    | `green` | Bright green variant |
    | `perez` | Inspired by Perez's frog (*Pelophylax perezi*) |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Condor (1 skin)">
    | Skin | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `base` | Classic Andean condor with black plumage and white ruff |

    |---|---|

<Note>
Condor and Chameleon each have only a `base` skin in the current release. Additional skins for these species may be added in future updates.
</Note>

## Teams

Nine teams are available, each named after or inspired by a faculty, group, or community connected to UNAL:

```gdscript game_constants.gd
enum Teams {
    NO_TEAM,
    ACETILES,
    ADN,
    INGENIOSOS_ELEMENTALES,
    PHOTO_AGROS,
    PLUMA_DORADA,
    RPC_TEAM,
    REAL_PINCEL,
    VA_GAMES,
    ZOOTECNICOS
}
```

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Acetiles">
    A team with roots in UNAL's engineering and technical sciences communities. The name plays on "acetylene" — a nod to chemistry and materials science.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ADN">
    Named after DNA (ADN in Spanish), this team connects to UNAL's biological and life sciences faculties. Science-forward and precise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ingeniosos Elementales">
    "Clever Elementals" — this team celebrates the elemental card system itself and the creativity of UNAL's engineering faculty students.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Photo Agros">
    A team tied to agricultural sciences and photosynthesis, representing UNAL's agronomy and environmental programs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pluma Dorada">
    "Golden Feather" — a poetic team name honoring the arts, humanities, and the Condor's golden plumage. Represents UNAL's arts and humanities tradition.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="RPC Team">
    Inspired by computing and systems engineering. RPC (Remote Procedure Call) is a nod to UNAL's systems and computer science programs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real Pincel">
    "Royal Brush" — a team celebrating visual arts, design, and UNAL's fine arts faculty. Players who choose Real Pincel value creativity and expression.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="VA Games">
    A team dedicated to video game development and interactive media, directly representing the creative team behind Beast Card Clash.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Zootecnicos">
    From UNAL's animal science and zootechnics program — fittingly, this team is all about the animals. A natural choice for players who lean into the wildlife theme.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Player defaults

When a new session starts, `PlayerStats` (an autoload) holds your current character configuration:

```gdscript player_stats.gd
# PlayerStats autoload — persists across scenes
var player_name: String = "Osorio"
var team: GameConstants.Teams   = GameConstants.Teams.NO_TEAM
var species: GameConstants.Species = GameConstants.Species.BEAR
var skin: String = GameConstants.SKINS[species][0]  # "base"
```

The default character is a Bear with the `base` skin and no team assigned. You can change all of these on the character selector screen before entering a match.

## Selecting your character

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the character selector">
    From the main menu, navigate to the character selector screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your species">
    Select one of the four species: Bear, Condor, Chameleon, or Frog. The available skins update automatically based on your selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your skin">
    Pick a skin from those available for your species. Your character preview updates in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your team">
    Select one of the nine teams (or leave it as `NO_TEAM`). Your team is displayed on the results screen at the end of a match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the battle">
    Confirm your selection and start the match. Your choices are saved to `PlayerStats` and persist for the session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your team choice and skin are purely cosmetic — they do not grant stat bonuses or affect which cards appear in your deck. Focus on your deck-building strategy and elemental composition to gain a competitive edge.
</Tip>
